How We Drive Change (Theory of Change)

Moving from immediate relief to structural educational empowerment to break the cycle of poverty.

01

Catching Them Young

Sponsoring school fees, uniforms, and books to take children off the streets of Lagos and secure their future learning.

02

Standing in the Gap

Targeted grassroots welfare distributions (staple foods, sanitary items) directly through local Baales and market heads.

03

Empathy in Action

Partnering with verified care homes to supply bulk provisions, medical assets, and infrastructure support.
